Frequently Ask Questions

Why should I do an internship?

Internships provide hands-on experience, networking opportunities, and a chance to explore different career paths. They help students apply theoretical knowledge, clarify career goals, and can often lead to future job opportunities.

When are internships offered and what is the duration?

We offer internships typically for 1 month or 3 months, depending on the student’s preference and project requirements.

Who can do an internship?

Generally university students, recent graduates, or career switchers, can apply for internships, provided they meet specific program or company.

What is a virtual (remote) internship?

A virtual (remote) internship is an internship program that you can complete online without physically going to an office. All tasks, projects, meetings, and mentorship are carried out using digital tools such as email, chat, video calls, and project management platforms.

Do I need prior experience to qualify?

A little prior knowledge is required, but you don’t need to be an expert. Basic understanding of the subject will help you get started, and our internship program will guide you to build deeper skills.

The typical career path after completing this internship?

After completing the internship, students often move on to advanced internships, entry-level jobs, freelancing opportunities, or higher studies in their chosen field. The skills and projects completed during the internship strengthen your portfolio, resume, and career prospects in industries like software development, app development, AI/ML, and more.

How do I apply for a remote internship?

You can apply by filling out our online application form or by submitting your details through email/google form. Once your application is reviewed, our team will contact you for the next steps.

How will my performance be evaluated remotely?

Your performance will be evaluated based on: Task Completion – Finishing assigned projects on time. Quality of Work – Accuracy, creativity, and problem-solving skills. Learning & Growth – How well you improve and apply new skills during the internship.

How do I stay connected with the team remotely?

You will stay connected through regular online meetings, chat groups, and collaboration tools (like Zoom, Google Meet, Slack, or Microsoft Teams). We ensure proper guidance and communication so you feel like a part of the team, even while working remotely.

Will I receive a certificate after completing the internship?

Yes, you will receive an official certificate of completion at the end of your internship. This certificate can be used to strengthen your resume, LinkedIn profile, and future job or academic applications.
